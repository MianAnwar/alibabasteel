Our Story

AliBabaSteel was founded in Lahore because we were frustrated with mass-produced imported desks that wobble, scratch easily, and cost Rs 40,000+ for mediocre quality.

We are a steel fabrication workshop that transitioned into furniture manufacturing. Our background is structural metalwork — not flat-packed furniture — and it shows in every weld.

Today we manufacture simple computer tables, standing desks with hand-crank mechanisms, custom multi-monitor workstations, and accessories — all under one roof in Lahore. Every desk is built to order, with configurable dimensions, frame finishes, and tabletop options.

We sell directly to end customers and small businesses. No retail markup. No middlemen.
